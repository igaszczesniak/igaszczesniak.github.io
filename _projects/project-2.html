--- title: "Satellite tracker" excerpt: "Real-time visualization of the AEROS MH-1 satellite's position." collection: projects ---

My company has been involved in a consortium to launch the Earth observation satellite AEROS-MH1, and I wrote the backend to track its position. I used orbital data from a TLE (Two-Line Element) file sourced from CelesTrak to build an orbit propagator with the SGP4 algorithm, then converted the resulting position vectors to the WGS-84 (World Geodetic System 1984) coordinate reference system.
