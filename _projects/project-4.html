OSIP is a platform run by the European Space Agency to collect and evaluate innovative ideas from academia, industry, and startups. Since its creation in 2018, it has gathered a valuable dataset of disruptive R&D project proposals from various aerospace domains. I developed an agentic workflow to cluster OSIP ideas by topic and identify emerging technologies within these clusters.

Honestly, I really enjoyed this project. I used the mistral-small-latest model from Mistral AI, which performed well in text summarization, labelling, assigning projects to relevant topics, and generating descriptions and insights.
