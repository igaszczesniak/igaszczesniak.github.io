OSIP is a platform run by the European Space Agency to collect and evaluate innovative ideas from individuals, companies, and research institutions. Since its creation in 2018, it has gathered a valuable dataset of disruptive space-related ideas from various domains. I have developed an agentic workflow to cluster OSIP ideas by topic and identify the emerging technologies within these topics.

Honestly, I really enjoyed this project. I used the mistral-small-latest model from Mistral AI, which performed well in text summarization, topic categorization, assigning projects to relevant topics, and generating descriptions and insights from trends.
