--- title: "OSIP trend summarizer agent" excerpt: "AI-powered clustering and trend analysis of ESA's OSIP R&D proposals." collection: projects ---

The OSIP platform collects innovative space R&D proposals from academia, industry, and startups. This agentic workflow ingests thousands of OSIP ideas, generates summaries, clusters them into thematic categories, tracks monthly trends, and produces insights for each category.

The pipeline is powered by Mistral Large for summarization, categorization, assignment, and narrative generation, with a custom scraper for OSIP data and a temporal analysis. Outputs include a web dashboard for interactive visualization.

I really enjoyed building this end-to-end system, which gave me a clear view of emerging trends across space hardware and software domains.
